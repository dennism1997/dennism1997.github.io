Basecamp!

Do you want to start the new season in perfect condition? Want to improve your volleyball skills?
Then subscribe for the BaseCamp! From the 6th up until the 10th of August 2018, D.S.V.V. Punch, SKC and Erasmus Volley
will organise a training week, dedicated to improving your volleyball skills.

Information

From the 6th up until the 10th of August, a volleyball training week will be organised in Delft. You will get two-hour training sessions from different trainers, twice a day for 5 days in a row. In this week you will receive a lot op tips and experience from the different trainers, you will become better and you will have new points to focus on in the start of your season. The first training of the day will take place in the morning and the second in the afternoon. In between training sessions a lunch will be prepared for you. One day you will start at 9.30 hours and you will be done at 15.30 hours, the other day you will start at 11.30 hours and you will be done at 17.30 hours. The different aspects of volleyball will be divided over the trainings, so you will have trained everything by the end of the week. To prevent overloading your muscles and possible injuries, the intensity of the trainings will be different for each training. In two of the trainings a part will be dedicated to power training. The groups will be formed according to volleyball level, to make sure you train with people of approximately the same level. If you are curious about the planning of this week, then scroll down and look at the draft of the scheme. schema.

The maximum number of places is 96 and members of D.S.V.V. Punch, SKC and Erasmus Volley have priority.

The costs for the complete week are maximum €65 p.p.. If you want to join, then scroll down and subscribe!

Update: We are working on the possibility to have one or two sport related clinincs next to the training sessions.
